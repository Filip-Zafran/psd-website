Frequently Asked Questions

How it works?

Our speed dating events consist of timed rounds (3-5 minutes) where you'll meet different people in a structured way. There are breaks between rounds to take notes and catch your breath. After all rounds, you'll submit an opt-in form indicating who you'd like to stay in touch with. We only share matches when there's mutual interest!

Application process (dates & info)

Check our Apply page for upcoming dates → Submit the Google Form → Wait for confirmation email (usually within 1 week) → Arrive prepared (phone charged for check-in, open mind ready). Space is limited so we recommend applying early.

How our software works?

Custom-built matching system: Check-in via QR code → Automated round assignments → Private opt-in matching → Mutual matches receive contact/social info via email after event.

Can I...
  • Arrive late? We accommodate late arrivals within first 30 mins when possible
  • Bring a friend? Yes, if they register separately
  • Skip a round? Yes, just let organizers know
  • Change pronouns on my badge? Absolutely, anytime!
