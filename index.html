What is PSD

Poly Speed Dating (PSD) is a community event that brings polyamorous and poly-curious people together in Berlin and online. Our format creates space for meaningful conversations and potential matches in an atmosphere of warmth, respect, and inclusivity.

We celebrate diversity of relationship styles, identities, and preferences, and we honor each participant's boundaries and feelings.

Our goal: to make connecting fun, safe, and authentic.
